About Me

I am a DPhil (PhD) researcher in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a member of Worcester College. My research is centered on 3D vision, robotics, and domain adaptation, especially for building robust perception in real-world settings.

Under the supervision of Prof. Niki Trigoni and Prof. Andrew Markham, I explore techniques such as group-equivariant learning, 3D pose estimation, and cross-domain generalization. Some of my work extends to interdisciplinary applications such as wildlife motion capture.
